Vertical Axis Wind Turbine with Inverter

This project involves designing and developing a Vertical Axis Wind Turbine integrated with an inverter.

Project Objectives:

  • Design a VAWT that maximizes energy capture.
  • Integrate a reliable inverter for efficient power conversion.
  • Analyze the performance of the system under varying wind conditions.

Components Used:

  • VAWT Type: Darrieus
  • Inverter: 500W DC to AC inverter
  • Sensors: Anemometer for wind speed measurement
  • Battery: 12V lead-acid battery for energy storage

Results:

The turbine achieved a maximum power output of 200W at a wind speed of 10 m/s.
